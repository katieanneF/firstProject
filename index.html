Angel of Death AC-130 Gunship

For about 50 years, versions of the aircraft, known as the “Angel of Death” for its lethality, have been providing close-air support, air interdiction and armed reconnaissance to troops throughout the world.

  • There are currently only 17 of these jets that are operational with the USAF.
  • The AC-130 is currently the world's largest flying artillery gunship.
  • It includes three-side firing weapons, a 25mm Gatling gun, a 40mm Bofors cannon, and a 105mm howitzer.
